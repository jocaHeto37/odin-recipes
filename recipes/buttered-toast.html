Buttered Toast

Description

This simple recipe takes the humble sliced bread and embellishes it with a profound depth of both flavour and texture.

(Description.)

Pain magnifique!

Ingredients

  • Sliced bread
  • Butter

Instructions

  1. Toast the bread to taste
    • Different breads toast differently
    • Quick, high intensity heat can char the outer layers
    • Slow, low intensity heat can make it crispy throughout
  2. Butter the toast to taste
